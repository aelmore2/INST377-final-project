About

Inkwell has a single goal: to provide lovers of classic literature with a centralized location to find, track, and store information about their favorite books and discover new favorites as well. While there are several services dedicated to facilitating the book-searching process, most are dedicated to modern literature, and those that aren't tend to have outdated user interfaces that are clunky, unintuitive, and hard to navigate. Inkwell aims to bring classic literature into the 21st century by offering readers a new way to discover a library of books that have been loved and admired for years!

  • You can navigate through this website audio:
    • Navigate through the pages:
      • Say: Navigate to {page}
    • On the search page, look for a specific work or works by an author:
      • Say: Search {input}
  • You will be able to navigate through four separate pages:
    • Home Page
      • This is your introduction to Inkwell. Ten popular books that you may enjoy are automatically populated when you open this page.
    • Search Page
      • On this page, you will be able to look up books by author or title.
    • Favorites Page
      • Here, you will find all of the books that you favorited in one central location.
    • Lucky Page
      • Test your luck! On this page, an assortment of random books from a random genre are generated and you may find one, or several, that peak your interest!
